The Body of Christ

A Church of Pentecost Ministry Serving God's Kingdom

Our Story

Founded on the principles of faith, love, and the power of the Holy Spirit, The Body of Christ ministry continues the rich tradition of the Church of Pentecost in serving God's people.

Our Heritage

Founded on the biblical pattern established in Acts 6, The Body of Christ ministry emerged from the recognition that as God multiplies His people, we must multiply our service. When growth brought challenges in caring for all members of our community, we heeded the apostolic wisdom to appoint Spirit-filled leaders of good report to oversee practical ministry. This allows our pastoral team to devote themselves continually to prayer and the ministry of the Word, while ensuring no one in our spiritual family is neglected in daily care. Like the early church, we believe that Spirit-led administration and devoted spiritual leadership must work in harmony to fulfill Christ's mission.

What We Believe

  • The Bible as God's inspired Word
  • Salvation through Jesus Christ alone
  • The baptism and gifts of the Holy Spirit
  • The Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
  • The Church as Christ's body
  • Water baptism for believers
  • Holy Communion as a memorial
  • Christ's second coming

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ through evangelism, teaching, and nurturing believers in the Pentecostal tradition, empowered by the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision

To see transformed lives and communities through the power of the Gospel, building a vibrant church that impacts generations for Christ.
